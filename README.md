This project showcases the remarkable contributions of 11 African American healthcare pioneers through an engaging, story-based web experience. Each innovator's section includes biographical information, key achievements, and professional portrait imagery.

### Featured Innovators

1. **Dr. Patricia Bath** - Pioneered laser cataract surgery and community ophthalmology
2. **Otis Boykin** - Invented improved pacemaker control units
3. **Dr. Kizzmekia S. Corbett-Helaire** - Led COVID-19 vaccine development at NIH
4. **Dr. Charles Drew** - "Father of the Blood Bank"
5. **Dr. Joycelyn Elders** - First African American U.S. Surgeon General
6. **Dr. Daniel Hale Williams** - Performed early successful open-heart surgery
7. **Dr. Percy Julian** - Advanced synthetic cortisone production
8. **Dr. Rebecca Lee Crumpler** - First African American woman to earn MD in U.S.
9. **James McCune Smith** - First Black physician with medical degree in U.S.
10. **Vivien Thomas** - Pioneered cardiac surgery techniques
11. **Dr. Miles V. Lynk** - Co-founded National Medical Association
